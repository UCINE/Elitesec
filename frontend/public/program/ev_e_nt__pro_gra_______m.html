ABOUT NULLHAT MOROCCO

NULLHAT Morocco is a national cybersecurity event that brings together university students and security researchers for collaboration, learning, competition, and live hacking. Over two days, participants will dive into expert talks, a high-stakes CTF, and hands-on hacking experiences alongside professionals, making it a unique space where knowledge meets practice.

Empowering Morocco's Cybersecurity Future

NULLHAT Morocco 2025 is designed to create a lasting impact on Morocco's cybersecurity landscape, bringing together university students nationwide for an intensive learning experience. Our strategic objectives include:

  • Empower students with high-quality cybersecurity challenges crafted from The Eliteâ€™s Security content.
  • Strengthen national collaboration among Moroccan universities and institutions
  • Raise cybersecurity awareness highlighting its importance for academic and professional growth
  • Expose students to professional tools and methodologies used in the cybersecurity field
  • Feature HackerOne partnership bringing global cybersecurity standards to Morocco
  • Host live hacking demonstration showcasing real-world security research

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NULLHAT Morocco 2025

What skill level is required for NULLHAT Morocco 2025?
All skill levels are welcome! The CTF competition features challenges across multiple domains with varying difficulty levels. Expert talks will provide insights for beginners and advanced participants alike. In addition, live hacking sessions with the experienced Moroccan bug bounty community will give participants the chance to learn, collaborate, and gain hands-on experience.
Do I need to bring equipment for the CTF?
we have set up everything needed for the event, but you can bring your own laptop with your preferred tools and setup for the best CTF experience.
Are meals and accommodation included?
Yes! Meals will be provided during both days of the event. Accommodation support is available for participants traveling from other cities across Morocco.
Is this event only for Moroccan university students?
NULLHAT Morocco 2025 primarily targets university students from across Morocco, with invited bug hunters joining for the live hacking demonstrations. This national event aims to strengthen the Moroccan cybersecurity community and foster collaboration among academic institutions and industry experts.
